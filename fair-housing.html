Equal Housing Opportunity

Our Fair Housing pledge and statement of policy.

Our Pledge

Resident Association Management is fully committed to the principles of equal housing opportunity. We are pledged to the letter and spirit of U.S. policy for the achievement of equal housing opportunity throughout the Nation. We encourage and support an affirmative advertising and marketing program in which there are no barriers to obtaining housing.

Federal Protections

The Fair Housing Act prohibits discrimination in the sale, rental, and financing of dwellings, and in other housing-related transactions, based on the following protected classes:

Race
Color
National Origin
Religion
Sex
Familial Status
Disability

In addition to federal protections, we honor and uphold all applicable state and local fair housing laws that may extend additional protections.

Our Practices

In every aspect of our community management work, we are committed to:

• Treating every homeowner, prospective homeowner, vendor, and visitor with dignity, fairness, and respect.
• Ensuring all communications, services, and decisions are free from discriminatory bias.
• Providing reasonable accommodations and modifications for residents with disabilities, in accordance with applicable laws.
• Maintaining accessible and inclusive community communications.
• Promptly investigating and addressing any complaints or concerns regarding fair housing.

How to Report a Concern

If you believe you have experienced housing discrimination, you may file a complaint with the U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD), Office of Fair Housing and Equal Opportunity (FHEO).
